Smart Mess Management System - Features Summary
  • AI-powered meal prediction based on weather, upcoming events, and user preferences
  • Multiple authentication methods (ID/Password, Face Recognition, RFID/NFC)
  • Attendance tracking and absence prediction
  • Nutrition monitoring and personalized recommendations
  • Pay-as-you-eat and digital wallet integration
  • Food waste tracking with gamification rewards
  • Pre-booking system to reduce food waste
  • Portion size adjustment to match appetite
  • Sustainability metrics and impact visualization
  • Quick meal voting system for menu planning
  • Admin dashboard with real-time analytics
  • NGO portal for food donation coordination
